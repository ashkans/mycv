## About FloodMapp

FloodMapp is a rapidly growing technology company with the important mission to build a safer future. Flooding is the costliest natural disaster, causing billions in damage and taking thousands of lives across the globe every year. FloodMapp delivers real-time flood intelligence to emergency managers, supporting them to prepare, respond and recover from flood events.

- **Stage:** Series A (~$9.9M raised, led by Union Square Ventures)
- **Founded:** 2016, Brisbane
- **Tech:** Real-time flood forecasting at the intersection of hydrology, hydraulics, automation and machine learning

## What They Are Looking For

They are seeking expressions of interest from candidates who can improve and expand their groundbreaking flood modelling system. Specifically, people with some combination of the following:

- Experience in hydrology, including model development and calibration
- Operational experience with real-time flood forecasting systems
- Understanding of machine learning and its application to hydrology with a strong foundation of mathematics and statistics
- 1D hydraulic modelling and 2D flood modelling experience with either MIKE21, TUFLOW or HEC-RAS
- Skills in GIS, data collection, manipulation and analysis
- Experience in Python software development, particularly with experience in data science toolkits such as pandas and scikit-learn
- Other support roles such as administration, HR, finance, marketing & sales, customer support or customer success
